Making traditional Japanese ramen from scratch is a labor-intensive process, but it's also incredibly rewarding. Here's a basic guide to help you create a delicious bowl of ramen:

### Ingredients and Equipment

#### Broth (Shoyu Ramen, Soy Sauce Ramen)
- **Base Ingredients:**
  - 2 lbs (1 kg) dried shiitake mushrooms
  - 2 lbs (1 kg) chicken bones
  - 2 lbs (1 kg) pork bones
  - 1 large onion, quartered
  - 2 carrots, quartered
  - 2 celery stalks
