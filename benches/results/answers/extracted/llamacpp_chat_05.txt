There are several reasons why your sourdough bread might be turning out too dense. Here are some common issues and potential solutions:

1. **Over-kneading or Under-kneading**: Sourdough bread requires a good knead to develop the gluten structure. If you're not kneading enough, the dough won't rise properly, leading to a dense texture.
