Making traditional Japanese ramen from scratch is a labor-intensive process, but it's also incredibly rewarding. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you create a delicious bowl of ramen:

### Ingredients and Equipment

#### Broth:
- **Base Ingredients:**
  - 2 lbs (1 kg) pork bones (chicken bones can be used as a vegetarian alternative)
  - 1 onion, quartered
  - 2 carrots, quartered
  - 2 celery stalks, quartered
  - 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  - 10 cups (2.5 liters) water
