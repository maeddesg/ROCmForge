
Munich is a fantastic city with a rich history, delicious food, and charming neighborhoods. Here's a suggested itinerary for a weekend trip:

**Day 1:**

1. **Morning: Marienplatz and Old Town**
   - Start your day at Marienplatz, the heart of Munich. Visit the New Town Hall (Neues Rathaus) and the Old Town Hall (Altes Rathaus) for their impressive architecture and historical significance.
   - Walk around the surrounding area, including the Glockenspiel, which performs at 11:00 AM and 12:00 PM.
