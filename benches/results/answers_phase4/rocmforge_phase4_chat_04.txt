
 Quantum computing is a fascinating and complex field that leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to perform computations. Let's break it down into simpler terms:

### Classical Computers vs. Quantum Computers

**Classical Computers:**
- **Bits:** These are the basic units of information in classical computers. They can be either 0 or 1.
- **Operations:** Classical computers perform operations on bits using logic gates (AND, OR, NOT, etc.) to process information.

**Quantum Computers:**
- **Qubits:** These are the basic units of information in quantum computers. Unlike classical bits, qubits can exist in multiple states simultaneously
